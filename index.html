Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our manufacturing and delivery.

Do you deliver outside of Richards Bay?
Yes, while we are local manufacturers, we provide delivery services across the KwaZulu-Natal region for bulk orders.
What is the standard lead time for IBR sheeting?
Most orders are manufactured and ready for collection or delivery within 3 to 5 business days, depending on current floor volume.
Can you manufacture custom lengths?
Absolutely. We manufacture IBR and corrugated sheeting to your specific measurements to minimize waste and onsite cutting.
Do you provide installation services?
We are strictly manufacturers and suppliers. However, we work with a network of trusted local contractors we can recommend.
